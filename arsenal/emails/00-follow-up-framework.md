# Follow-Up Email Framework

## Two-Tier Strategy

### Tier 1: Real ICP Fit
*Owner/Founder with a genuine operational breakdown, decision authority*

**Goal:** Full diagnosis + proposal
**Effort:** 30–45 minutes per person
**Timeline:** Send by Wednesday after event (April 29)

**Structure:**
1. Personal opening (specific detail from their submission)
2. One specific observation about their problem (the diagnosis)
3. What solving it could look like (high-level only)
4. Next step (brief call to explore? or send proposal?)
5. Your personality (keep it real, not sales-y)

**Tone:** Problem-solver, not salesperson. You're confirming you understand their specific situation.

---

### Tier 2: Warm Acknowledgment
*Everyone else (non-ICP, curious, referral-seeding, future potential)*

**Goal:** Stay in touch, build relationship, create referral possibility
**Effort:** 5–10 minutes per person
**Timeline:** Send batch on Thursday (April 30)

**Structure:**
1. Thank you for coming
2. Brief line about what you're doing (ICP-focused)
3. "I'm focused on [specific problem]. If that's you, we should talk. If not, I'd love to stay in touch."
4. One question that feels genuine (not sales-y)
5. Soft close

**Tone:** Honest and warm. You're not pitching. You're building a network.
