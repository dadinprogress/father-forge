# Email Template: Follow-Up

**Category:** Follow-up  
**Use case:** 7 days after initial outreach with no response  
**Expected open rate:** 30-35%  
**Follow-up window:** 3 days

---

## Template

**Subject:** Re: Quick question about operations

---

Hi [First Name],

Following up on my note last week about [Problem Area].

I realize you're probably swamped, so I'll keep this short:

If you're open to a 20-minute conversation about how [Competitor/Similar Company] tackled [Problem Area], I've got specific frameworks that tend to work.

No pressure either way.

Let me know.

[Your Name]

---

## Customization Guide

**[First Name]:** Their first name (again)  
**[Problem Area]:** Same as original email  
**[Competitor/Similar Company]:** A company they know (can be real or relevant)  
**[Your Name]:** Your name

---

## Success Metrics

- Open rate target: 40-50% (higher than first email)
- Reply rate target: 3-8%
- If no response by follow-up, stop the sequence

## Notes

- Subject line should reference original email
- Shorter than first email (under 100 words)
- Give them an out ("no pressure")
- Two follow-ups max (don't become spam)
- If no response after this, move on
- Save these contacts for future campaigns
