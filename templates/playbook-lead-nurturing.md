# Playbook: Lead Nurturing Sequence

**Category:** Playbook  
**Use case:** Leads who aren't ready to buy yet  
**Timeline:** 30 days  
**Expected conversion:** 15-25%

---

## Overview

This playbook outlines a 30-day email + content sequence to keep warm leads engaged until they're ready to move forward.

---

## Sequence

### Day 1: Welcome
**Email:** Short introduction, link to your best case study  
**Goal:** Establish credibility  
**Expected open rate:** 35%

### Day 5: Value Drop
**Email:** Share a framework or tool they can use immediately  
**Goal:** Show you understand their world  
**Expected open rate:** 25%

### Day 10: Social Proof
**Email:** Testimonial from similar company + brief story  
**Goal:** Build trust through peer validation  
**Expected open rate:** 22%

### Day 15: Educational Content
**Email:** Link to article, video, or guide on their pain point  
**Goal:** Position you as expert  
**Expected open rate:** 20%

### Day 20: Soft Pitch
**Email:** "Still thinking about this?" + offer of brief call  
**Goal:** Give them permission to move forward  
**Expected open rate:** 18%

### Day 25: Social Proof #2
**Email:** Another client story (different angle)  
**Goal:** More proof points  
**Expected open rate:** 18%

### Day 30: Final Call
**Email:** "Last chance to chat" + deadline  
**Goal:** Create urgency without being pushy  
**Expected open rate:** 20%

---

## Rules

- **Send at consistent times** (9 AM their timezone on Tuesdays/Thursdays)
- **Personalize each email** (not template sends)
- **Don't be salesy** (give first, ask second)
- **Track opens and clicks** (identify hot leads)
- **Segment out non-responders** (after day 15, if unopened 2x, cut frequency)

---

## Metrics to Track

- Open rate (target: 20%+)
- Click-through rate (target: 5%+)
- Reply rate (target: 2%+)
- Conversion rate (target: 15%+)

---

## Variations

**High-intent leads:** Compress to 14 days, more pitch-heavy  
**Cold leads:** Extend to 45 days, more education  
**Multiple decision makers:** Customize content per role

---

## When to Stop

- After 2 unopened emails in a row → weekly instead of every 5 days
- After 30 days with no engagement → archive for 3 months
- After explicit "no thanks" → stop immediately
