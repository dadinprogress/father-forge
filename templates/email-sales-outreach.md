# Email Template: Sales Outreach

**Category:** Sales  
**Use case:** Initial outreach to warm leads  
**Expected response rate:** 15-20%  
**Follow-up window:** 5-7 days

---

## Template

**Subject:** Quick question about your operations — [Company Name]

---

Hi [First Name],

I was checking out [Company Name]'s work in [Industry], and I noticed something:

Most [Company Type] struggle with [Problem Area] — it's usually the gap between [Current State] and [Desired State] that kills growth.

I work with founders and operators like you to find that one thing silently breaking the operation, then build a fix that actually sticks. Takes about an hour to figure out.

No pitch. No obligation. Just a conversation.

Would this be worth 20 minutes next week?

[Your Name]  
[Title]  
[Contact Info]

---

## Customization Guide

**[Company Name]:** Research the actual company  
**[Industry]:** Their sector (SaaS, manufacturing, services, etc.)  
**[Company Type]:** What they do (agency, startup, consultant, etc.)  
**[Problem Area]:** The specific challenge they likely face  
**[Current State]:** Where they are now  
**[Desired State]:** Where they want to be  
**[Your Name]:** Your actual name  
**[Title]:** Your title (e.g., Operations Strategist)  
**[Contact Info]:** Email, phone, or calendar link

---

## Success Metrics

- Open rate target: 35-45%
- Click rate target: 15-25%
- Reply rate target: 5-10%

## Notes

- Keep it short (under 150 words)
- Make it specific to them (research required)
- No attachments in first email
- Personalization increases open rate by 45%
- Follow up if no response within 7 days
